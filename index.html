First Conditional – British English Lesson

The first conditional describes real and possible future situations.

Cloze Test (15 Questions)

1. If you ______ the door quietly, nobody will notice.
2. She’ll finish the project on time if she ______ focused.
3. If they ______ the map properly, they won’t get lost.
4. We’ll have a picnic if the weather ______ nice tomorrow.
5. If he ______ his homework now, he’ll be free later.
6. You’ll miss the train if you ______ too slowly.
7. If the cat ______ the window open, it will jump out.
8. We’ll buy snacks if the shop ______ open.
9. If you ______ your boots, your feet will stay dry.
10. He’ll be cross if we ______ the match without him.
11. We’ll go straight home if it ______ dark early.
12. I’ll lend you my notes if you ______ them back tomorrow.
13. She’ll join us later if she ______ her work early.
14. They’ll be delighted if we ______ the good news.
15. I’ll message you if the bus ______ early.

Correct Answers

  1. close
  2. stays
  3. read
  4. is
  5. does
  6. walk
  7. sees
  8. is
  9. wear
  10. start
  11. gets
  12. bring
  13. finishes
  14. tell
  15. arrives
